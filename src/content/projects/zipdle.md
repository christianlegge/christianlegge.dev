Zipdle is essentially backwards Wordle, but I can't really claim credit for coming up with it. I have a friend who started to do the Wordle not to find the word, but to draw cool patterns with the squares. I thought "you know what, I bet that's harder than it looks," and I figured it would make a neat puzzle.
